Our Services

What We Do


Physical therapy can help you get back to your regular life whether that is sports, work, exercise routines or just daily activities. We help our patients recover from injuries or surgeries and get them back to feeling their best.

  • Physical Therapy: Physical therapy can help treat new injuries or chronic pain by treating the underlying cause and fixing imbalances in the body that can help prevent the pain from returning.
  • Hand Therapy: Our hand therapists can help you relieve pain from conditions such as arthritis or carpal tunnel and get you back to your normal routine.
  • Sports Medicine: Our Sports Specialists will work with you to get back to your favorite activities whether that be walking or extreme sports.
  • Dry Needling: Dry Needing is a theraputic modality that targets specific points in a muscle to help that release tension and relieve pain.
